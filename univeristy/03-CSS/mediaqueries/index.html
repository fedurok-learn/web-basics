Diversity

Across all 5 kingdoms, there are an estimated 8.7 million species of animals. However, about 99% of all species that have existed are said to be extinct.

Threats

Current pollution levels are causing major harm to animal inhabited areas.

Protection

Global animal protection organizations are working constantly to establish new safe habitats for animals on the brink of extinction.

Support

You can support animal protection by donating to organizations, as the funds help them to continue to work diligently.
